Malam Kebersamaan Arthindo Group 2026

We were commissioned to orchestrate this strategic corporate gathering, designed to help the company announce its early-year milestones and introduce new business partners. Our goal was to facilitate a seamless communication channel between leadership, stakeholders, and the internal team.

To effectively appreciate stakeholders and the internal team, we merged the concepts of a corporate town hall and an entertainment showcase. This event delivered two key values: disseminating important, confidential internal updates while providing engaging entertainment for all employees.

After presenting several concepts to leadership, 'Malam Kebersamaan Arthindo' was selected as the winning theme.
